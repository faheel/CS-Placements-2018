About Jamia Millia Islamia

Jamia Millia Islamia was founded at Aligarh in the United Province, India in 1920 during the Khilafat and Non-Cooperation Movement in response to Gandhiji's call to boycott government supported educational institutions.
Jamia moved from Aligarh to Delhi in the year 1925. Jamia was made a central university by an act of Parliament in 1988. Today, Jamia Millia Islamia is one of the most prominent and promising Central Universitities of the country.

About Department of Computer Science

The department of Computer Engineering was started in the year 2000. Cruising through the initial turbulent year, the department has evolved to become one of the leading departments at Jamia Millia Islamia.
The department offers two undergraduate courses - B.Tech and B.E in Computer Engineering. Both the courses offer theoretical expertise complimented by industry relevant training. Apart from the curriculum, the department has actively organized lectures for its students on variuos technical and non technical topics delivered by professionals from the industry.
The department also runs the Ph.D program successfully, under which a lot of research scholars are working in the feild of Networking, Data Mining, Artificial Intelligence etc.
